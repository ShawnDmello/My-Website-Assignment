-------"ABOUT SPORTSHUB"-------

Introduction

SportsHUB is governed by its constitution, and run by a Council of formally elected volunteers. The Council meets regularly to discuss and make decisions on behalf of the members and is elected each November at the AGM. The club is run day to day by our employed Managing Director, Shawn D'mello. One of the key attractions to many members, be they social or players, is the venue. The Club is in the heart of Village, in an almost countryside atmosphere, with good local transport, free parking and a genuine family atmosphere.

Sport Club Council

Community Sports Hubs are an exciting and innovative approach to the development of sport in local communities. They focus on sustainable, community-led approaches that get clubs working together to develop welcoming, safe and fun environments for sport They bring together sport clubs and community organisations who want to develop and grow the sporting offering in the community
